Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 June 2026  ·  Governing law: Hong Kong SAR

Spotter ("the app", "we", "us") is a fitness-tracking app with an AI pose-analysis coach, workout planner, nutrition logger, and AI health coach. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how it is used and shared, and your rights under the Hong Kong Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (Cap. 486) ("PDPO").

1. Data Controller

The data controller is the operator of Spotter, contactable at support@spotterhk.com. All requests relating to personal data should be directed to this address.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide, personalise, and improve app features (AI coaching, workout tracking, nutrition logging, plans, progress charts)
  • To sync your data securely across your devices
  • To calculate health estimates (BMI, TDEE, BMR, recommended protein) displayed within the app
  • To generate AI coaching responses and food/body-composition analysis via our backend
  • To understand how the app is used and improve it (analytics)
  • To detect, diagnose, and fix crashes and technical issues
  • To respond to support or data requests you send us

4. Data Sharing & Third-Party Processors

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We share data only with the following service providers, each of whom processes data solely to provide their service:

Our AI proxy server strips identifying information before forwarding requests to the AI provider. AI provider keys are stored server-side and are never shipped in the app binary.

5. Data Security

  • All network traffic is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
  • Your account data in Supabase is protected by row-level security — only you can access your own records
  • Passwords are hashed by Supabase Auth and never stored in plain text
  • AI provider credentials are held server-side only
  • The original workout video file is never uploaded — only a small number of extracted still frames are sent, and only when you use the AI Form Coach

6. Data Retention

  • Account & fitness data: retained for as long as your account exists
  • Workout video frames: transmitted for AI form analysis and not stored by us beyond generating that response
  • AI request content: not stored beyond the duration of the API call
  • Analytics data: retained per PostHog's standard policy
  • Crash reports: retained per Sentry's standard policy
  • On account deletion: all your records (profile, workouts, sessions, weight, nutrition, water, coaching history, diagnostics) are removed from our database immediately and irreversibly

7. Hong Kong PDPO — Your Rights

Under the Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (Cap. 486), you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data held by us
  • Correct any inaccurate personal data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Object to processing in certain circumstances

To exercise these rights, use the in-app features (Profile → Edit Profile; 我的 → 刪除帳戶) or contact us at support@spotterhk.com. We will respond within 40 days as required by the PDPO.

🗑️ Account deletion: Go to 我的 (Profile) → 刪除帳戶 (Delete Account) in the app. Type "刪除" to confirm. This immediately and irreversibly removes your account and all associated data from our servers.

8. Apple HealthKit

If you grant permission, Spotter reads step count and heart-rate data from Apple HealthKit. This data is displayed on your home screen only. It is not uploaded to our servers, shared with third parties, or used for advertising. We do not write any data back to HealthKit. You can revoke this permission at any time in iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Spotter.

9. Children

Spotter is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will revise the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
