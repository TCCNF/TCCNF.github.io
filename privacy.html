Spotter — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 June 2026

Spotter ("the app", "we") is a fitness-tracking app with an AI pose-analysis coach. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and your choices.

1. Information we collect

  • Account: your email address (for sign-in), via our authentication provider Supabase.
  • Profile & fitness data: name, age, gender, height, weight, goals, training preferences, workout logs, training sessions, weight history, and AI-coaching records. Stored on your device and synced to your private account in Supabase.
  • Camera & microphone: used only on your device for real-time pose detection and optional workout-video recording. Video is processed locally and is not uploaded to our servers.
  • AI coaching & chat: text describing your exercise (e.g. joint angles, rep counts) and your chat messages are sent through our secure backend to a third-party AI provider to generate feedback.
  • Usage analytics: app events (screens viewed, features used, sign-ups, logins) collected via PostHog to help us improve the app. This may include session interaction data.
  • Crash & error reports: diagnostic data (device model, OS version, error stack traces) collected via Sentry when the app encounters an error.
  • Diagnostic & performance data: app version, device model, OS version, and language, plus AI-request performance (which feature, response time, and whether the AI or an offline fallback answered). Linked to your account and used only to keep the app running well.
  • Feedback: messages you choose to submit via the in-app feedback form.

2. How we use your data

  • To provide app features (tracking, plans, AI coaching) and sync your data across devices.
  • To understand usage and improve the app (analytics).
  • To detect, diagnose, and fix crashes and bugs.

3. Data sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with the service providers that operate the app: Supabase (account, database), our AI provider (to generate coaching responses), PostHog (analytics), and Sentry (crash reporting). Each processes data only to provide their service.

4. Data security & access control

Your account data is protected by row-level security so that you can only access your own records. Network traffic uses HTTPS. AI provider keys are kept on our server and are never stored in the app.

5. Your rights

You can edit your profile in the app and sign out at any time. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data directly in the app: go to 我的 (Profile) → 刪除帳戶 (Delete Account). This immediately and irreversibly removes your account and all your records (profile, workouts, sessions, weight, nutrition, AI-coaching history, and diagnostics) from our database. You may also email us at the address below to request deletion.

6. Children

Spotter is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your region).

7. Changes

We may update this policy and will revise the date above.
